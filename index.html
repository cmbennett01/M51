About the Image

(mine, not the HST image)

Equipment

  • Telescope: 14in f8 LX850 SCT
  • Camera: Atik 383L mono CCD
  • Filters: Baader LRGB filter set

Image Frames

  • 5 x 900s subframes in each LRGB filter
  • 4 master flats, each a comb of 11 x 2s subframes
  • 1 master dark, a comb of 11 x 900s subframes
  • 1 master bias, a comb of 200 subframes

Image processing

Subframes were calibrated, registered and integrated with the master flats and darks.

The final image is an RGB combination of the integrated subframes.

Image processing

Didn't use the luminance data and didn't do any post-processing other than a histogram stretch and a little color calibration.

Mostly because I have no idea what I'm doing.
